About Me

Photography:

Hi, I’m Haley Clark, a Utah-based photographer with a passion for capturing life’s meaningful moments. I specialize in photographing families, couples, kids, and portraits, creating images that feel natural, timeless, and full of personality.

Whether it’s documenting the joy of family connections, the love between couples, or the unique character of individuals, my goal is to help you feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera. I believe every session should be fun, relaxed, and true to you—so the photos we create together become memories you’ll cherish for years to come.
